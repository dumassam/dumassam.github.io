Spawner-recruit modelling of salmon population dynamics.

This project is supervised by Dr. Marty Krkosek of the Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at the University of Toronto.

During the 2024-2025 academic year, I am joining the Krkosek lab to explore the utilization of spawner-recruit models for salmon population dynamics. This project is being conducted alongside the Lewis Research Group of the University of Victoria.
